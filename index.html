About Me

I am a research scientist at Sony Group Corporation (SGC) in Tokyo, with a concurrent role at Sony Interactive Entertainment (SIE). My work focuses on multimedia networked systems, with long-running interests in adaptive video streaming, real-time streaming, congestion control, and federated learning. I received my Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University, where I was advised by Prof. Lifeng Sun.
